What is AkademiX?

A MOOC platform based on Open edX

Developed at Western Norway University of Applied Sciences and the University of Bergen

You'll become familiar with the platform during the "hands on" part of the workshop

Based on (essentially) the same software running edX.org

Flexible: supports text, video, interactive content, advanced assessment, exams, discussion forum, wiki, ...

Learning analytics

Highly scalable: running on Amazon Web Services (which basically runs the entire internet at this point..)

Can get a lot of inspiration from courses on edX.org

Open for enrollment!
