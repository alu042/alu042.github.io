$f$ periodisk med periode $T=2L$.
Fourierrekke:

$ \begin{align} a_0 + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} a_n \cos\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right) + b_n \sin\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right), \end{align} $


der

$ \begin{align} a_0 &= \frac{1}{2L} \int_{-L}^L f(x) dx \\ a_n &= \frac{1}{L} \int_{-L}^L f(x) \cos \left(\frac{n \pi x}{L}\right) dx\\ b_n &= \frac{1}{L} \int_{-L}^L f(x) \sin \left(\frac{n\pi x}{L} \right) dx \end{align} $
