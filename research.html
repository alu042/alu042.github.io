Research

My primary research interests are related to algebraic and combinatorial aspects of geometric numerical integration, image processing and stochastic modelling. Some key tools and concepts: the combinatorics of trees and partitions, pre-Lie and post-Lie algebras, Hopf algebras, Lie group integrators, Lie–Butcher series, and flows on homogeneous manifolds.

More details can be found in the papers linked to below. A convenient starting point is perhaps the survey paper On algebraic structures of numerical integration on vector spaces and manifolds, or these slides.

I'm a member of ICT Engineering (also called DISTECH) at Bergen University College, a NILS project on discrete mechanics and geometric integration, and a project involving mathematical image- and signal processing, sponsored by UH-Nett Vest. A (work in progress) website can be found here: computationalmedicine.no.

Collaborators include Hans Munthe-Kaas, Kurusch Ebrahimi-Fard, Dominique Manchon, Pierre Del Moral, Simon Malham and Anke Wiese.
