Teaching

Current courses

I'm currently teaching MAT100 - Grunnleggende matematikk for ingeniører at Bergen University College. It's an introductory course in mathematics for engineering students, covering calculus, some differential equations, numerical methods and a bit of linear algebra. The website for the course is here. Unfortunately it's only accessible for students enrolled in the course.
