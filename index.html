Alexander Lundervold

I'm an Associate Professor of mathematics in the ICT Engineering group at the Department of Computing, Mathematics and Physics, Bergen University College.

My research activities are part of the Engineering Computing Group at HiB, and is primarily focused on algebraic and combinatorial structures appearing in numerical analysis, image processing and stochastic modelling.
